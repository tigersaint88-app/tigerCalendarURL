Overview

Tiger Lunar Calendar is designed as a practical lunar calendar tool. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

By default, we do not require an account.

  • User-provided settings (e.g., zodiac preference, reminder settings) are stored locally on your device.
  • If you enable notifications, the app uses your device’s notification system to deliver reminders.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use third-party analytics or tracking by default.

If you later add analytics (e.g., Firebase Analytics), update this policy and your App Store data disclosure accordingly.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

Data Storage

Settings and calendar reminders are stored locally on your device (e.g., using local storage / database). We do not upload your data to our servers by default.

Children

This app is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@yourdomain.com.
